Research

Current Research Interest

  • Environmental and human health sciences

Past Research

  • Phytoremediation of heavy metal contaminated soil and water.
  • Isolation and purification of hematopoietic stem cells from human bone marrow by monoclonal antibodies and immunomagnetic microspheres. Study of the effects of recombinant hematopoietic growth factors on the development and differentiation of the CD34+ progenitor cells in suspension culture by morphology, and immuno-phenotype characterization (using flow cytometry)
  • Locomotion, physical development, and brain myelination in rats treated with ionizing radiation in utero
  • Beer drinking, ethanol metabolism and development of physical dependence in rats treated with beer.
